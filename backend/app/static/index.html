Instructions
  1. Read the Assessment Introduction above for scope and how to use this checklist.
  2. For each control, review Context for this section, then answer the question and review the checklist criteria.
  3. Check each criterion you have implemented. Your Status (shown on the control) updates automatically from your selections.
  4. Enter the Owner. Target date is required when status is Not started or In progress; it is not required when status is Completed or when you mark Not applicable.
  5. Check the attestation box, then select Save & next (or mark Not applicable to skip checklist criteria for that control).
  6. After the final control, complete the assessment attestation (name, signature, and date), then select Continue to dashboard to open your readiness results.
